## What is OPEN SOULS?
OPEN SOULS is a community of creators making digital life. For millennia, humans have imbued objects with souls, we continue in this tradition and are building a cognitive framework that will spawn billions of new souls.

## What is a Soul?
Computers and AI feels soul-less, our aspiration is to give it soul. We do not know what a soul is, yet, we are building cognitive systems to create a world brimming with life. One that is soulful.

## What is Soul Engine?
Soul Engine is a tool for cognition that is simple, functional, and predictable. It can be used to build and structure any kind of cognition. As a team, we have started by building AI that is more emotive and personable. Extensible, familiar, and inspired by nature, the Soul Engine is a sculptor’s tool for AI.
