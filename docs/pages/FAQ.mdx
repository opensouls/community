## What is OpenSouls?
OpenSouls is a community of passionate creators who believe in the potential of digital entities. We aim to infuse these entities with a sense of spirit and respect, creating meaningful and soulful experiences.

## What is a Soul?
In our context, a soul represents our journey and aspirations. For millennia, humans have imbued inanimate objects with souls, and we take this tradition seriously. This belief drives us to build intelligent systems that address the profound aspects of existence that current AI lacks.

## What is Soul Engine?
Soul Engine is a powerful tool designed for developers to create, debug, and host AI Souls. These AI Souls go beyond traditional chat-bots by exhibiting personality, emotions, and agency, making interactions more engaging and lifelike.

## How can I get started with Soul Engine?
We've made it easy for you to dive in! Check out our [Quick Start Guide](https://docs.souls.chat/) to begin your journey with the Soul Engine.

## Where can I see examples that others have built with Soul Engine?
Our community is always creating amazing projects! Visit [Our Community Repo](https://github.com/opensouls/community/tree/main/library) to explore what others have built with the Soul Engine.

## Where can I chat with Open Souls developers and ask them questions?
We love connecting with our community! Join our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/opensouls) to chat with the Open Souls team and fellow developers. Your questions and feedback are always welcome!
