## What is OPEN SOULS?

OPEN SOULS builds tools vital for the creation of new digital life.

## What is a Soul?

A Soul is what you build with OPEN SOULS, an interface for the memory, cognition, desires and wishes of a digital being or entity.

## What is The Soul Engine?

The Soul Engine is a tool for steering machine cognition that is simple, functional, and predictable. It can be used to model any kind of thought, feeling, or mind. As a team, we began with building AI that is more emotive and personable.

Extensible, familiar, and inspired by nature, Soul Engine is a sculptor’s tool for AI.

## What can I use OPEN SOULS and The Soul Engine for?

OPEN SOULS can make your websites, images, art and intellectual property come alive! The Soul Engine is the tool for the job when your Creation requires a little more life.

## Who is using OPEN SOULS today?

Our users include developers, designers, hobbyists and game masters building for their own users.
